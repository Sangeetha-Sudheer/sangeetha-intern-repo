Professionalism Reflection

Professional vs Unprofessional Behaviour

Professional Behaviour
-	I will communicate respectfully as well as professionally with my teammates.
-	I will be on time for meetings. Respond to messages within a reasonable time.
-	I will listen to others’ ideas. Give them encouraging feedback on their work.
-	I will take accountability for my mistakes. Learn from them.
-	I will work well with my teammates. Support them when they need it.
-	I will keep on company rules. Keep intimate information private.

Behaviour
-	I will not be impolite or rude to my colleagues.
-	I will not disregard messages or miss meetings minus telling others.
-	I will not disturb people. 
-	I will not guilt others for my mistakes.
-	I will not use language or aggressor anyone.
-	I will not make conflict or extent negativity.

Respectful Communication in Remote Teams
When I communicate with my team, I will be polite and professional. I will reply quickly and clearly. I will also listen prudently during meetings. Respect different opinions. If I do not understand something I will ask questions. 
Giving and Receiving Feedback Professionally
When I give response, I will focus on things that can be better. I will elucidate what wishes to be better. Giving suggestions in a respectful way.
When I take feedback, I will listen carefully. Ask questions if I do not understand. I will not get defensive. I will use the feedback to improve my skills.
Reflection
Have you ever. Witnessed great teamwork and professionalism? What made it effective?

During my university group projects the best teamwork occurred when everyone communicated openly deadlines, collective responsibilities then supported each other. We talked to each other frequently. Respected each other, which assisted us work well together.
What steps can you take to make sure your communication is clear, respectful and inclusive?
-	I will think afore I send messages.
-	I will use professional language.
-	I will respect opinions as well as backgrounds.
-	I will ask for clarification if I am not sure about something.
-	I will keep my teammates knowledgeable about my progress or delays.
-	I will be open to feedback and different perspectives.

How can you help create a fun and professional work environment?
I can help by developing an attitude supporting my teammates celebrating our achievements communicating respectfully facilitating others when I can and treating everyone with kindness then professionalism.
How can you prepare effectively for meetings with supervisors besides colleagues?
Before meetings I will:
-	Review my notes and tasks.
-	Prepare updates on my progress.
-	Write down any questions or things that are blocking my work.
-	Join meetings on time with everything I need.
-	Take notes during the conversation. Follow up on action items afterwards.

What would it look like to be proactive during your internship?
Being means taking the initiative asking questions when I am not sure completing my tasks without being reminded looking for ways to improve processes and telling my supervisor if I have any issues.
How might you follow up with colleagues or supervisors if you need information or action? How would you increase it if you don't receive a response?
I will first direct a follow-up message after waiting for a reasonable amount of time. If I still do not receive a response besides it affects my work I will contact my supervisor or another team member clarifying the situation in a way then providing the necessary context.
What are some things you won't do?
- I will not. Bully my colleagues.
- I will not ignore feedback. Get defensive.
- I will not do poor-quality work on purpose.
- I will not share company information.
- I will not discriminate against anyone.
- I will not blame others for my mistakes.
- I will not behave unprofessionally during meetings or team discussions.
