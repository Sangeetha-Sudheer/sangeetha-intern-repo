\f1\b\fs48 \cf2 App Summaries
\f0\b0\fs32 \

\f1\b\fs36 \cf2 Freedom
\f0\b0\fs32 \
\pard\pardeftab720\sa213\partightenfactor0

\f2 \cf2 Freedom is a cross-platform distraction blocker with users over 3.5 million people. Its main feature is blocking websites along with apps across all devices simultaneously like phone, tablet, and desktop, using a single account. It offers scheduled and recurring focus sessions, a "Locked Mode" that blocks early exit.
\f0 \
\pard\pardeftab720\sa213\partightenfactor0

\f1\b\fs36 \cf2 Cold Turkey
\f0\b0\fs32 \
\pard\pardeftab720\sa213\partightenfactor0

\f2 \cf2 Cold Turkey is a desktop-only (Mac and Windows) distraction blocker known for being extremely difficult to avoid once a block is activated, it cannot simply be uninstalled to escape a block. Unlike Freedom, it has no mobile app support.
\f0 \
\pard\pardeftab720\sa213\partightenfactor0

\f1\b\fs36 \cf2 Tiimo
\f0\b0\fs32 \
\pard\pardeftab720\sa213\partightenfactor0

\f2 \cf2 Tiimo is a visual planning app made specifically for neurodivergent users with ADHD and Autism, though it works for anyone who aids from visual structure. It won iPhone App of the Year at the 2025 App Store Awards. Its best features include a color-coded visual timeline, per-activity countdown timers, an AI Co-planner, mood check-ins, besides widgets for glanceable schedule views. It focuses on reducing time blindness plus supporting task initiation, not on blocking distractions. 
\f0 \
\pard\pardeftab720\sa213\partightenfactor0

\f1\b\fs36 \cf2 Routinery
\f0\b0\fs32 \
\pard\pardeftab720\sa213\partightenfactor0

\f2 \cf2 Routinery is a step-by-step routine timer that walks users through their habits one task at a time, with a countdown timer for each step. It has features like voice alerts, auto-next, skip/pause/adjust controls, white noise, habit streak tracking, a plant badge reward system, besides lately added location-based reminders (e.g. trigger an evening routine when you arrive home). It is extensive with ADHD users besides families, plus won Apple's App of the Day in 2025. Free with a premium tier.
\f0 \
